Abstract : In this study, the nature and representation of 
news published in two Bangladeshi newspapers have been 
analysed using the ideas of the peace journalism model 
given by the Norwegian social scientist Johan Galtung. 
Here the coding frames are prepared in light of the peace 
journalism model for framing analysis; besides this, the 
content analysis method is used. Considering the overall 
results of the research, it is found that the Bangladeshi 
media are still dependent on the traditional style of 
journalism in reporting war and conflicts. Peace journalism 
practice has not been found on a large scale till now. Except 
for some events, the Bangladeshi media are practicing war 
journalism on a large scale in almost every case. The 
journalism practice of Bangladesh doesn’t show any 
evidence of coming out the conventional trends; rather, it 
shows the trends of sustaining the conflicts and contributing 
to shaping the problem to a greater degree. However, it has 
been proven in peace journalism practice that the media can 
play a vital role in resolving conflicts and establishing 
peace.
